OUR PROCESS

Manuscript Uploading By Client
Client is requested to send the file of manuscript which requires a book cover to give it its final look. Yes it is said, not to judge a book by its cover, but cover does wonders in marketing and developing readers interest in it.
01
Options For Covers
Our team of experts go through the book for a better understanding about the genre and what audience the author is focusing to target and show some relevant samples for a reference.
02
Format, Graphics, and Copy
The designers then designs the most unique and stylish design accommodating the clients need. Depending on what format the book will be printed, or in the form of an e-book. It may include front and back cover copy. Infusion of graphics along the design such as publishing company or author logos.
03
Client Verification
We then send the designed to our client for approval or any further modifications required to done.
04
Submission of The Cover
Book cover once approved by the client is ready and added to the book. Book is then sent for the next step which is publishing.
